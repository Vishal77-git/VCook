title: Leftover Khichdi Tikki
prepTime: 10m
cookTime: 15m
servings: 3
category: Zero Waste
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
nutrition:
  calories: 200
  fiber: 3g
  protein: 6g
  vitaminA: 11%
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ingredients:
  2 cups leftover khichdi
  1 boiled potato (mashed)
  2 tbsp chopped coriander
  1/2 tsp garam masala
  chili powder to taste
  salt to taste
  bread crumbs (optional)
  oil for shallow frying
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
instructions:

Mix leftover khichdi with mashed potato and spices.
Add coriander and combine well.
Shape into small patties.
Coat with breadcrumbs if desired.
Shallow fry until crispy and golden.
